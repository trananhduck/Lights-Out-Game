Game Rules

  • You are provided with an 8x8 matrix of squares and a target image of the same size.
  • Your task is to click on squares to change the Game Board to match the Target image.
  • When you click on a square, it and the four adjacent squares (above, below, left, right) will change color (from White to Black and vice versa).
  • Try to solve 10 levels as quickly as possible. Good luck!
